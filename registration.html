Registration

Please select the RuleML option on the ECAI On-line registration system. RuleML participants can attend all ECAI 2014 sessions at the same conditions as ECAI 2014 delegates. Our fees are the same registration fees that are announced on the ECAI 2014 website including special student fees. The ECAI 2014 staff will put RuleML Springer printed proceedings into ECAI 2014 conference bags for RuleML registrants.
